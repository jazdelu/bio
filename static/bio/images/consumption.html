5 Reasons to consume Organic & Fair Trade Products
  1. 1. Safer: Chemicals pesticides and additives free. It has being proved: consuming traditional food is full of chemical pesticides, among us, 60% of herbicides, 90% of fungicides, and 30% of insecticides which are carcinogenic

Why Organic?
With increasing awareness of nutrition, food safety and environmental friendly products among consumers, organic food is becoming more and more popular in people’s daily life. The organic food market in China will continue to grow at rates of 30% over the next decade in overseas markets as well as domestic.
