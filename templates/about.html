About Us
Bio In-Bev is the first French company developing a full range of Organic and Fair trade beverages.
Our mission is to offer a range of the highest quality beverages at the most competitive prices possible, ensuring to people living in China safe and healthy premium* products with genuine taste and aroma.
Bio In-Bev is striving to offer consumers better quality beverages from people who make the beverages better by strictly selecting every supplier in France.
If it’s Eco-friendly for the Earth and the farmer, it’s also Eco-friendly for the consumer.
Our commitment is to deliver safe and qualitative products as well as offering relevant range that make a real different to our customers, consumers and stakeholders from France to China.
We believe the best way for us to do this, is through deep mind awareness on sustainability while having passion for what we do.
*Premium products differentiate themselves with ingredients and craftsmanship
Our Philosophy
Founded in early 2014, Bio In-Bev is a spin-off of Shanghai Wangdu Trade, French Wine Wholesaler in China.
Lionel and Julie, the two founders, developed a simple concept full of common sense:
We have French products here. A bit strew in several companies.
But, do we have a reference French company; imported only a range of organic and safe products? And, how to verify if it's certified organic?
The concept of Bio In-Bev was born.
We created the first French company offering a full range of French Organic and Fair trade beverages that enables Healthy & Eco-conscious people in China to drink tastier and safer!
Few months, many tastings, two trips in France later among campaigns, vineyards, offices, across Paris, Cognac, Bordeaux, Languedoc, Lyon, Rhone Valley and Burgundy, Bio In-Bev is glad to introduce you today the best of the French Authentic and Innovative beverages... in a total transparency.
